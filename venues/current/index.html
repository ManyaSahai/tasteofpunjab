Overview

Innovative and modern, Current combines unique design elements and textures to create a contemporary 6,000-square-foot setting for a wide range of events. This sophisticated, state-of-the-art venue is ideal for weddings, bar/bat mitzvahs, corporate events, and holiday celebrations for up to 360 guests.

Features

  • 16-foot custom-lit led ceiling
  • Floor-to-ceiling windows
  • Outdoor waterfront access
  • 800-square-foot balcony
  • Private suite with restroom, safety box and vanity
  • Blackout and solar window shades
  • Multiple rigging points
  • On-site audiovisual specialist
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Ground-floor access for ease of production load-in and display vehicles
  • Valet parking and shuttle bus services available
  • 4 EM Acoustics speakers – delivering optimal sound to every guest seat
  • Industry leading Dante AV over IP network – providing uncompressed digital audio, massive channel counts & infinite signal routing flexibility
  • 2 Panasonic 10,000 lumen laser projectors – offering high contrast, vibrant color video & computer presentations
  • 2 Da-Lite 7.25’ X 11.6’ motorized tension screens in 16:10 aspect ratio
  • Combination of Chauvet professional “Rogue” LED intelligent moving head lighting fixtures – providing a perfect balance of movement, color & pattern textures to enhance every performance
  • Multiple ETC Source4 ellipsoidal tungsten Instruments – highlighting a perfect stage
