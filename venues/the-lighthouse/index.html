Overview

Evoking a sophisticated Manhattan loft space, The Lighthouse offers a unique waterfront setting boasting 10,000 square feet of versatile event space featuring sleek hardwood floors and distinctive lighting fixtures. Whether it’s a picturesque sunset wedding ceremony overlooking the Hudson River or an intricately designed multi-day conference, The Lighthouse is the perfect backdrop for any event up to 800 guests.

Features

  • Fully Renovated (January 2022)
  • Spacious all-season, glass-enclosed terrace with outdoor waterfront access
  • Floor-to-ceiling windows
  • Two private suites with restrooms, safety boxes and vanities
  • Solar window shades
  • On-site audiovisual specialist
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Valet parking and shuttle bus services available
  • 16 QSC ADS 112 speakers combined with 5 Atlas 12” ported sub-woofers – delivering the ideal balance of audio from a lectern microphone, DJ or live musical performance
  • Industry leading Dante AV over IP network – providing uncompressed digital audio, massive channel counts & infinite signal routing flexibility
  • 2 Panasonic 10,000 lumen laser projectors – offering high contrast, vibrant color video & computer presentations
  • 2 Da-Lite 7.25’ X 11.6’ motorized tensioned screens in 16:10 aspect ratio
  • Combination of Chauvet professional “Rogue” LED intelligent moving head lighting fixtures – providing a perfect balance of movement, color & pattern textures to enhance every performance
  • Multiple ETC Source4 ellipsoidal tungsten lighting instruments – highlighting a perfect stage
