Our Story

The Pier Sixty Collection has become a landmark for special events in New York City. Following the great demand for Pier Sixty after its opening in 1998, the business soon expanded two years later when it opened The Lighthouse on the neighboring pier. In 2015, the company was thrilled to add a state-of-the-art venue, Current, to its family of properties.

A true culinary experience

With a passion for providing world-class food and impeccable service, The Pier Sixty Collection offers innovative menus and presentations to create an unrivaled guest experience.
