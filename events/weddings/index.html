Your Dream Wedding Awaits

Celebrate your love story at The Pier Sixty Collection, New York City’s most iconic waterfront venues. Located along the Hudson River in the trendy Chelsea neighborhood, our three distinct venues—Pier Sixty, The Lighthouse, and Current—offer breathtaking views, sophisticated spaces, and an unforgettable backdrop for your special day.

Whether you envision an intimate ceremony under the stars or a grand reception with up to 2,500 guests, our flexible venues can be customized to bring your dream wedding to life. From the first toast to the last dance, we’re here to make every moment extraordinary.

The Experience

The Pier Sixty Collection Wedding Experience is centered on creating a seamless and enjoyable journey for every couple. We’ve thoughtfully curated an array of distinctive services and amenities, all designed with one central goal in mind – to simplify your planning process. 

When it comes to the often intricate task of arranging seating, our partnership with Prisimm ensures efficient coordination, ensuring it’s both swift and perfectly organized. 

But that’s not all! Through our exclusive collaboration with Kleinfeld Hotel Blocks, you gain access to a remarkable team of professionals dedicated to finding the ideal hotel room block for you and your guests. They’ll present you with options, prices, and concessions that are available exclusively through us. 

And the advantages don’t stop there! Imagine having your very own Bridal Attendant, a customized wedding cake, and so much more. At The Pier Sixty Collection, we can accommodate weddings with up to 850 cherished guests, ensuring that your special day is nothing short of extraordinary. 

Are you ready to explore further? Dive into our Venues Section and discover the endless possibilities that await. Your dream wedding is just a step away!
