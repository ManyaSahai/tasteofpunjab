Meetings that create Impact

Elevate your meetings and conferences at The Pier Sixty Collection, New York City’s most iconic waterfront venues. Located along the Hudson River in the vibrant Chelsea neighborhood, our venues—Pier Sixty, The Lighthouse, and Current—combine modern design, breathtaking views, and state-of-the-art amenities to create the ideal environment for professional gatherings.

From executive meetings and training sessions to multi-day conferences and panel discussions, our flexible venues are tailored to meet the needs of events of all sizes. With innovative technology, seamless service, and chef-curated menus, The Pier Sixty Collection ensures your meeting or conference exceeds expectations.

Elevate Your Conference: Unleash Potential

Welcome to the ultimate destination for conferences in the heart of New York City. At The Pier Sixty Collection, we specialize in transforming meetings and conferences into extraordinary, tech-driven experiences. Whether it’s trade shows, townhall meetings, trainings, investor gatherings, or bank meetings, we provide the perfect canvas for success.

A Venue Like No Other:
Imagine hosting your event in a space filled with natural light and breathtaking views of the city skyline. Our floor-to-ceiling windows create an inspiring atmosphere that reenergizes attendees, fostering creativity and innovation. Our outdoor spaces offer a refreshing escape, perfect for networking and brainstorming.

Cutting-Edge Technology:
With our in-house AV partner, KVL, your program and presentations will leave a lasting impact. We provide state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment and technical support to ensure that your message shines through with clarity and precision.

Seamless Connectivity:
Whether you’re conducting a powerful hackathon or facilitating high-stakes investor meetings, our robust Wi-Fi capabilities can handle it all. We understand the importance of staying connected in today’s tech-driven world, and we’ve got you covered.

Why Choose Us:
– Versatile Venues: From intimate Current to expansive Pier Sixty or The Lighthouse, our venues are adaptable to suit your specific needs.

– Service Excellence: Our experienced team is dedicated to providing top-notch service, ensuring that your event runs smoothly from start to finish.

– Culinary Experience: Fuel creativity with gourmet cuisine crafted by our world-class chef, tailored to your event’s preferences.

– Prime Location: Located in the heart of NYC, we offer convenient access for attendees, making your event even more accessible.

Experience Tech Excellence:
Join us at The Pier Sixty Collection and unlock the potential of your next Tech conference. We’re here to help you inspire, connect, and innovate. Contact us today to start planning an event that will leave your attendees amazed and motivated to reach new heights.
