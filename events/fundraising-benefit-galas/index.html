Host Exceptional Galas

Elevate your next gala or fundraising event at The Pier Sixty Collection, New York City’s most iconic waterfront venues. With breathtaking Hudson River views, outdoor, versatile event spaces, and world-class service, our venues set the stage for unforgettable evenings that leave a lasting impact.

Whether you’re planning an elegant black-tie affair or an innovative fundraiser, our venues—Pier Sixty, The Lighthouse, and Current—offer flexible layouts and modern design to accommodate events of all sizes. Paired with our award-winning catering and expert event planning team, your gala is guaranteed to impress donors and inspire generosity.

Elevate Your Cause

At The Pier Sixty Collection, we consider it both an honor and a privilege to host non-profit organizations dedicated to making the world a better place. For 25 years, we’ve stood beside those who fight tirelessly for noble causes, and we’ve watched with pride as Galas that once started in our intimate Current space have grown to grand events at The Lighthouse and Pier Sixty, accommodating up to 1200 guests.

🤝 Partnering for Impact: We understand that your mission is not just an event—it’s a commitment to change. Our team is committed to being your partner in creating a Gala that not only raises vital funds but also leaves a lasting impression. Your cause becomes our cause, and we’re dedicated to helping you succeed.

🏆 A Legacy of Service: With a quarter-century of experience, we’ve honed the art of service timing and perfected the art of extraordinary cuisine. Our culinary team crafts menus that leave a lasting mark on your guests, turning your Gala into an unforgettable dining experience.

 

✨ Why Choose Us:

  • Unmatched Venues: From the intimate and elegant Current to the spacious grandeur of The Lighthouse and Pier Sixty, our venues offer versatility to accommodate the scale of your Gala.
  • Service Excellence: We understand the importance of seamless execution. Our professional staff is adept at ensuring every moment of your Gala runs smoothly.
  • Culinary Brilliance: Our world-class chefs create culinary delights that will delight your guests, leaving them with a taste of excellence to remember.
  • The Destination for Benefit Events: We are proud to be the destination in New York City for Benefit Events, offering unmatched settings and service for those who strive to make a difference.

 

Join Us in Making a Difference: Together, let’s make your Gala an instrument of change. We invite you to experience The Pier Sixty Collection, where your cause is at the heart of everything we do. Contact us today to start planning an impactful Fundraising & Gala Event that will leave a lasting legacy.
